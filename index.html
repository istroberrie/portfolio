FAQ

Common questions

How long does a project take?
Depends on scope. Smaller projects - sequences, basic workflows - take two to three weeks. Larger builds like a full CRM cleanup or multi-step funnel take four to eight weeks. I scope everything upfront so there are no surprises.
What if I don't know GoHighLevel well?
That's fine. I handle the build. You get a working system, clear documentation, and a walkthrough so your team knows exactly what to do. You don't need to know GHL deeply - that's what I'm here for.
Do you offer ongoing support after the project?
Yes. Projects include handoff and training. Beyond that, I offer monthly support for tweaks, optimization, and troubleshooting. Many clients also add VA support once their systems are running.
Can you fix an existing GHL setup that's already broken?
Yes. Cleanup and audit work is a big part of what I do. If your current setup is messy, confusing, or broken, I'll audit it, identify what's working and what isn't, and rebuild what needs to be rebuilt.
Do you also do VA work alongside GHL?
Yes. Inbox management, calendar, social posting, and admin support. Some clients start with automation and add VA support once systems are in place - others do both from the start.
What if I'm using a different platform?
GoHighLevel is my core focus and where I produce the best results. I can work with other platforms, but GHL clients get faster and more reliable outcomes. Let's talk during discovery if you're on something else.
